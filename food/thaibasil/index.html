Thai Basil Indiranagar Bengaluru
Award winning restaurant for Authentic Thai food

Had heard a lot about the fantastic flavors of authentic Thai food.
So visited Thai Basil , Indiranagar, Bengaluru to taste some authentic Thai food.
Thai Basil has won many awards from Times for authentic Thai food
Ambience inside is soothing and peaceful , good relief from hurly-burly outside in indiranagar
I had ordered papaya green salad, spice level 3 which is a mix of noodles, raw papaya and lots of chilies.
Do keep in mind that Indian spice levels are much lower compared to Thai spice levels.
Better if you go for spice level 1 or 2 if you are sensitive to spicy food.
Pad Thai fried noodles with prawns was tasty.
I would say Caramel milk bubble brown sugar is a must try drink or desert in their menu.

Here is a vlog on my experience in Thai Basil Indiranagr Bangalore

Overall the experience with Thai food at Thai Basil was good and I am going to visit this place again.
