What it’s like to fly in Emirates Business Class

We had traveled in Emirates business class to and fro from Bengaluru to Dubai.
Emirates business class has all the luxury that anyone wishes for !!
They offer a complimentary chauffeur service for pick up and drop from airport for business class travelers.
Chauffeur service is provided by Blacklane . They will send a luxurious car at our convenient time for pickup.
Business class travelers are provided with complimentary international lounge access in Bengaluru Kempegowda International Airport.
Here is a vlog on our experience in international lounge in Bengaluru Kempegowda International Airport.
Lounge with beautiful greenery around ,offer a relaxing time with mouth watering food and variety of drinks.


Here is a vlog on our chauffeur ride in Bengaluru and in-flight experience in Emirates business class.

We had an excellent in-flight experience , with seats recline nearly flat , fantastic drinks , nice food and some linkedin learning videos.
They have a good selection of movies to watch as well.
Staff were really friendly and take care of our smallest requests.

On the way back to Bengaluru, we had a great time in Dubai Airport Terminal 3 , Business class lounge as well.
We opted to stay in Asian section of the lounge where there were a huge variety of Indian snacks and main course items.
The sweets were just yummy which had our very own indian sweets like Rasmalai ,Gulab Jamun.
Lounge has facility to sleep and relax as well before the flight.

Thank you Emirates for making our journey safe , effortless and luxurious !!!
See you again.
